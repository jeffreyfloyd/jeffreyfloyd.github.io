I am a data scientist, a strategic problem solver, and a voracious learner. My background in the supply chain industry challenged me to approach unique situations
flexibly and provide solutions to a wide range of needs. I am passionate about mission driven work and looking to support companies trying to make a positive impact.

I've been a lover of games and technology for as long as I can remember. When I was three years old my uncle put me in front of his PC and let me play SkiFree and
Asteroid, and I've been hooked ever since. Eventually I built my first PC when I was 14 so I could bring it to LAN parties and play games with my friends. Growing up
on the internet has been a magical experience where I get to learn about anything that crosses my brain at a moments notice. During the pandemic I started watching
cooking shows on Youtube and now making meals for my friends has become another of my favorite hobbies!
